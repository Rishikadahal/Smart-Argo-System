We Create
Awesome Work

Our idea was to help people enthusiasts in agriculture by creating a small system that can check the moisture level of the soil and water the plant when needed. The system integrates various hardware components such as Arduino, NodeMCU, and soil moisture sensor.

The process of connecting and configuring these components was complex, but it allows us to gain a deeper understanding of how these technologies work together. We have work closely together to ensure that everything is running smoothly and efficiently.

Though we faced challenges in making the components work together seamlessly, we were determined to make it happen. The end result was a system that can be controlled remotely through a website, which makes it easy for people to monitor their plants even from far away.

Our Service
Soil moisture monitoring system can continuously monitor the moisture levels of the soil and provide real-time data to farmers, allowing them to make informed decisions about when to water their crops.

The system can also be integrated with irrigation systems, allowing it to automatically water the crops based on the soil moisture levels and user input.

Similarly, the system can transmit data to a server using an API, allowing farmers to monitor the health of their crops from anywhere, at any time.
